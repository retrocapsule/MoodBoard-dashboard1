Strain Description

White Runtz is a potent hybrid marijuana strain made by crossing Gelato and Zkittlez. This strain is celebrated for its remarkably fruity flavor profile that smells just like a bag of candy. It features a sweet and tingling high that will have you feeling relaxed and euphoric.

Genetics
