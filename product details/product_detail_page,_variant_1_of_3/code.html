Genetics

Gush Mints is a potent indica-dominant hybrid marijuana strain made by crossing Kush Mints with F1 Durb and Gushers. This strain produces a strong, euphoric high that will leave you feeling happy and relaxed. Gush Mints has a complex aroma with notes of pine, sweet berries, and mint. It is an ideal choice for an after-work strain or for anyone looking to unwind and chill out.
