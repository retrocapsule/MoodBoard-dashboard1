Born from an illustrious lineage, Gush Mints is a masterfully crafted indica-dominant hybrid, a direct descendant of the legendary Kush Mints, F1 Durb, and Gushers. This genetic symphony creates a potent and deeply satisfying experience, renowned for its ability to guide users into a state of profound euphoria and tranquil bliss. Prepare for an olfactory journey as Gush Mints unveils a complex and captivating aroma. Initial notes of crisp pine dance with an undertone of sweet, pungent diesel, elegantly balanced by a refreshing hint of cool mint. Upon combustion, the flavor profile blossoms, delivering a rich, creamy, and undeniably gassy taste that caresses the palate and leaves a memorable, lingering finish, inviting you to savor every moment.
